About Us

Established in 1998, Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a premier engineering institution affiliated with JNTU Kakinada. Located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, PVPSIT is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

With a sprawling campus equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. Our experienced faculty and robust academic programs ensure that students are prepared for global challenges in their careers.

At PVPSIT, we emphasize research, industry collaboration, and extracurricular activities, creating a well-rounded learning experience. Join us to embark on a journey of academic excellence and holistic development.
